What to expect

Simple, reverent worship centered on Christ.

Sunday mornings at Fillmore Christian Church are intentionally straightforward: we gather, open Scripture, pray, sing, share the Lord's Table, and encourage one another in the hope of Christ.

Whether you are new to church, returning after time away, or looking for a congregation near Fillmore, you are welcome here.

Arrive

Sunday School starts at 9:00 AM.

Choose from classes for preschool and elementary children, middle and high school students, or adults. Worship begins at 10:00 AM in the sanctuary.

Expect

Scripture, prayer, singing, and communion.

The service is reverent and uncomplicated, with preaching from the Bible at the center.

Family

Children are welcome in worship.

If you are bringing kids or have accessibility questions, send a note and we will help you plan.

Before you come

A few simple details.

  • Dress in a way that helps you worship comfortably.
  • Bring a Bible if you have one; if not, you can still follow along.
  • Children are welcome in worship throughout the service.
  • Questions are welcome before or after the service.
