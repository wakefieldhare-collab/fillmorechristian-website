About Us

Getting to know Fillmore Christian Church

Fillmore Christian Church is a non-denominational church established in 1865. We believe Jesus is the Christ, the Son of the living God. We believe the Bible is our rule for faith and practice in the new birth through Christ alone.

We are a welcoming community of believers in Fillmore, Missouri, committed to growing in our faith together, serving our neighbors, and sharing the love of Christ with all.

Whether you've been a Christian for decades or are just beginning to explore faith, you're welcome here. We invite you to join us on Sunday mornings for worship and fellowship.
