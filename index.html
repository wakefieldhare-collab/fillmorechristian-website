Established in 1865

Worshiping Christ in Fillmore for generations.

Fillmore Christian Church is a small-town congregation centered on Scripture, prayer, fellowship, and the good news that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of the Living God.

First time at Fillmore?

Come as a neighbor. Worship with us as family.

Sunday mornings are intentionally simple: gather, open Scripture, pray, sing, share the Lord's Table, and encourage one another in Christ.

Arrive

Sunday School starts at 9:00 AM.

Worship begins at 10:00 AM in the sanctuary at 310 N. Florence Street.

Expect

Scripture, prayer, singing, and communion.

The service is reverent and straightforward, with preaching from the Bible at the center.

Family

Children are welcome in worship.

If you are bringing kids or have accessibility questions, send a note and we will help you plan.

What to expect

A historic church with a living hope.

Our services are simple and reverent: Scripture read and preached, prayer, congregational singing, and the Lord's Table as a response to the Word.

Whether you have followed Christ for years or are just beginning to ask questions, you are welcome here.
